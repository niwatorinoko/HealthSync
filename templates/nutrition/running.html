Running Nutrition Guide

Oatmeal

Provides a slow and steady release of energy, avoid mid-run crashes, ensures stable blood sugar levels, promote endurance.

Bananas

Bananas is ideal for pre-run snacks since they offers a quick energy boost , prevents muscle cramps and maintains electrolyte balance

Sweet Potatoes

Loaded with vitamins and complex carbs, they replenish glycogen stores post-run.

Lean Protein
(Chicken, Fish)

Chicken and fish provide high-quality protein along with other nutrients like zinc and omega-3s, which reduce inflammation and support recovery.

Why These Foods?

These foods fuel endurance, support recovery, and maintain energy levels, crucial for both short and long-distance running.
