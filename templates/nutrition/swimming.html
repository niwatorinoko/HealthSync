Swimming Nutrition Guide

Salmon

Rich in omega-3 fatty acids, salmon helps to reduce inflammation and supports joint health. Its high protein content also aids muscle repair after rigorous sessions.

Quinoa

Complete protein containing all nine essential amino acids, quinoa provides sustained energy and aids recovery.

Coconut Water

A natural source of electrolytes like potassium, magnesium, and sodium, coconut water helps maintain hydration levels, reducing the risk of cramps and fatigue.

Pasta
(Whole Grain)

Whole grain pasta provide the energy needed for endurance in the pool. Its high fiber content also supports digestive health.

Why These Foods?

These foods support endurance, recovery, and hydration, which are vital for swimmers who engage in rigorous training.
