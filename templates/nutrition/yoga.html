Yoga Nutrition Guide

Chia Seeds

These tiny seeds are power-packed with omega-3 fatty acids, which reduce inflammation, and fiber, which promotes digestive health.

Spinach

A rich source of iron and magnesium, spinach helps combat fatigue and supports muscle function and relax muscle.

Almonds

High in healthy fats, protein, and vitamin E, almonds boost energy and enhance brain health, supporting focus during yoga practice.

Berries

Loaded with antioxidants like vitamin C and polyphenols, berries fight oxidative stress caused by physical exertion.

Why These Foods?

These foods promote flexibility, energy, and recovery, essential for yoga practice, which emphasizes mindfulness and physical endurance.
