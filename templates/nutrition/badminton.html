Badminton Nutrition Guide

Whole Grain Wraps

Whole grain wraps are an excellent source of quick-digesting carbohydrates, providing immediate energy and supporting blood sugar stability.

Greek Yogurt

High in protein and calcium, Greek yogurt aids in muscle repair and helps maintain bone strength. Its probiotics support digestive health.

Fruits
(Oranges, Apples)

These hydrating fruits are rich in natural sugars, offering quick energy bursts. Their high vitamin C content boosts immunity as well.

Nuts
(Walnuts, Cashews)

Provide a combination of healthy fats, protein, and magnesium. They offer sustained energy and reduce muscle fatigue, ensuring endurance.

Why These Foods?

These foods enhance agility and endurance, providing the necessary fuel for quick movements and recovery in badminton.
