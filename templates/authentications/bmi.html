WHO BMI Classification

BMI Value Classification
Less than 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Normal weight
25 - 29.9 Overweight
30 - 34.9 Obesity Class I
35 - 39.9 Obesity Class II
40 and above Obesity Class III
Note: BMI is a general guideline and may not account for all body types. For a comprehensive health evaluation, consult a healthcare professional.
